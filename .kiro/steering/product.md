# Product Overview

This is a KPI dashboard application for P.Borges restaurant operations, tracking performance metrics for 2025. The dashboard provides comprehensive analytics across multiple business areas:

- Sales performance and growth tracking
- Delivery operations and cash sheet management
- Service times and operational metrics
- Cost analysis and inventory management

- Utilities consumption monitoring (water, electricity)
- People management and labor metrics
- Maintenance and operational expenses
